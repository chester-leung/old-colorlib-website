About Chester

Hey. I'm Chester. I'm currently a student at Cal, studying computer science. Near the end of high school, I became fascinated by the power of technology and the potential efficiency of product development — an idea you think of only minutes before can be turned into a tangible product you can show your friends and family in just a few days or even a few hours.

At Cal, I perform with the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra and the UC Berkeley Chamber Orchestra. I've also immersed myself in the quantum computing field with Quantum Computing at Berkeley.
This semester, I'm serving as a consultant to Zivit, an early stage startup, alongside other consultants at Seed Base Consulting.

For as long as I can remember, I've loved sports-- I started off playing baseball and basketball, focused on volleyball in middle and high school , and now play intramural volleyball, basketball, and dodgeball. I'm also an avid Giants and Warriors fan — been following them since their dark years, from 2005 onward.

Currently, I'm interested in the debate surrounding the (future) impact of superintelligence on humankind, the emergence of augmented reality in everyday life, and the development of autonomous vehicles. Recently, I've made a habit out of watching TED talks to learn more about these and other areas. Though my art skills are mediocre at best, I'm also exploring the wonders of graphic design.

Hit me up if you want to talk tech or play pickup — I'm also pretty much down for almost anything else. Go Bears!
